Credit Me |   SH-C-CM  


Use

This label should be assigned when a creator wants to be credited for their content.

Details

Creators should have the option to be credited when their content is being reused from the archive. Collectors and creators should work together to determine the best form of credit - handle, full name, link to social media, email notification.

Design

Plants have names! Identification can be useful.
