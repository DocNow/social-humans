Anonymize Me |   SH-C-AM  


Use

This label should be assigned when a creator wants to be anonymous.

Details

Anonymity is a form of protection, but it is hard to achieve. Collectors and creators should work together to determine what level of anonymization is needed on a case-by-case basis. Perhaps simply removing or unlinking a handle will suffice, making it more onerous for a user to find the person. Perhaps multiple tweets can be combined into a composite tweet, so that exact text cannot be searched on the originating platform to find the creator.

Design

Plants need screening from bugs and pests, and creators need protection from bad actors doxxing or stalking them.
