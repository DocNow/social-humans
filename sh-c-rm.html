Remove Media |   SH-C-RM  


Use

This label should be assigned when a content creator requests that images be removed from archived content.

Details

Creators may want their words archived, but not the attached images. This could be for privacy reasons, or if they were not the original creators of the image.

Design

Flowers may be cut to create temporary bouquets, but the plant lives on. Images may have contemporaneous value but not be considered archival by the creator.
