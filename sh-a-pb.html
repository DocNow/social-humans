Prob-a-Bot |   SH-A-PB  


Use

This label should be assigned when an account is likely a bot. Indicate % likelihood in label.

Details

There are various tools that you can use to determine the probability that an account is a bot. Things to consider include rate of tweeting, timestamps of tweets, stock images such as national flags, long strings of numbers in user handles, and more.

Design

Bots aren't alive! They should be made more visible. Not all bots are bad, but noting their existence is important for context.
