Expire Access |   SH-C-EA  


Use

This label should be assigned when a creator requests that access to this content expire.

Details

In some situations, a content creator may want their materials to be archived temporarily but not be a part of a permanent record. Collector and creator should work together to determine the appropriate archiving period, and materials should be removed from the archive after that period has passed.

Design

Some plants are annuals, they exist to illuminate the world for just a season. Similarly, not all content is meant to live forever.
