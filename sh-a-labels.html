SH-A Labels

These labels are meant to be used by archivists and academics building social media collections.

Consent Granted

Creator has consented to having material archived.

Status: DRAFT

Delay Access

Creator has indicated access should be delayed for a period of time.

Status: DRAFT

Expire Access

Creator has indicated content should be removed after a period of time.

Status: DRAFT

Remove Location

Creator has indicated geolocation data should be removed.

Status: DRAFT

Remove Media

Creator has indicated images and video should be removed.

Status: DRAFT

Remove Network

Creator has indicated network of connections should be removed.

Status: DRAFT

Anonymize Me

Creator has indicated a desire to be anonymized.

Status: DRAFT

Credit Me

Creator has indicated a desire to be cited and credited in publications.

Status: DRAFT

These labels are in DRAFT form and are open for comment and suggestions. Please contact for comments or to nominate a new label.
