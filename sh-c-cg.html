Consent Granted |   SH-C-CG  


Use

This label should be assigned when a creator grants consent to use of their content.

Details

Consent should be defined and negotiated between collector and creator. Ideally, consent is an ongoing process, and the archive should make provisions for continued consent and the ability to revoke consent.

Design

Planting seeds is the first step to nurturing growth. Opening a conversation by asking for consent is the first step to a respectful and ethical archive.
